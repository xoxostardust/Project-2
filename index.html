The era of surveillance capitalism.

According to the Pew Research Center, 79% of American adults are concerned about how companies use the data they collect from them. Another 81% revealed they feel that they have no control over how and which data is collected by companies. There is good reason to, as well, considering how often surveillance capitalism is used in the online world. However, many are not even aware of the term that keeps their data in a chokehold online. It's time for a change. A change through educating, securing, and protecting.

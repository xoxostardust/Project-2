Have I Been Pwned?

Have I Been Pwned is notably one of the most popular websites that can be used to check the security of one’s data from breaches. By typing in one’s email, he or she has access to information about any data breaches the email was connected to through accounts or anything similar. The website can also tell users what exactly was released, such as passwords, personal information, and more.

NordPass

Created by the same company that brought users NordVPN, Nordpass offers a mobile way to track one’s passwords, as well as managing them securely. Additionally, the application also has a data breach scanner which reveals if a user has been hacked or currently uses vulnerable passwords. It is important to note that, unlike both of the above websites, NordPass does cose money. Considering all that the application does to protect one’s data, though, it is definitely worth the price.
